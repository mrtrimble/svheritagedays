Antique Automobiles

The Sherman's Valley Heritage Days has a car show every year that displays classic and antique automobiles. Early model Ford cars and trucks, as well as muscle cars and more!

Farm Equipment

Farming is the backbone of the Sherman's Valley, and the equipment we use is quite important. Typically we have plenty of Tractors and other farm equipment on display to learn about!

Gas Engines

Come see our gas engine displays including Hit and Miss Gas Engines and learn about how they were used in Farming and the Farming communities.

Mountain Men

Visit our Mountain Men re-enactment where you can learn about how the Mountain Men of the Sherman's Valley hunted and scavanged to survive.

Sherman's Valley Heritage Days, Inc. is a non-profit organization which was founded in 1986. Its purpose is to demonstrate and preserve the role of antique farm equipment, vehicles, hand-craftsmanship, and the heritage of the way things were done in the "Good Old Days", and to educate the public about preserving items and practices from the past.

Featured Cuisine

Good Country Food! A wide selection of country foods will be available throughout the weekend, including delicious sausage sandwiches, roast pork sandwiches, and scrapple to take home.

Hog Maw Dinners

A country favorite, made with pork, potatoes, vegetables, and other fresh ingredients that make a delicious meal.

Kettle Soups

A Sherman's Valley Heritage Days standard, we serve up open kettle cooked Chicken Corn and Ham and Bean Soups.

Scrapple

We also offer up this Pennsylvania Dutch tradition, fried to perfection and served on-site or available to go!
